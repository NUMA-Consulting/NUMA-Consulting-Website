Who We Are

A student-powered
marketing consultancy

Founded in 2021, NUMA Consulting (NUMAC) is a student-run marketing consulting agency at Northeastern University dedicated to delivering strategic marketing solutions.


As a subsidiary of the Northeastern University Marketing Association (NUMA), NUMAC specializes in consulting services, partnering with clients to address their marketing challenges and implement impactful strategies — at no cost to you.

"Our consultants bring real-world co-op and internship experience from leading organizations, channeling that expertise into meaningful marketing work for our partners."
